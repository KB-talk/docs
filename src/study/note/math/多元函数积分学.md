## 旋度与散度

旋度：
$rot F =\begin{vmatrix} i & j & k\\ \frac{\partial }{\partial x}  & \frac{\partial }{\partial y} & \frac{\partial }{\partial z} \\ P & Q & R\end{vmatrix}$

散度：
$div F = \frac{\partial P}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial Q}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial R}{\partial z}$
